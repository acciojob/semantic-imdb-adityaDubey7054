Plot Summary

In a future where Earth is becoming uninhabitable, former NASA pilot Cooper is recruited for a daring mission. He must pilot a spacecraft through a newly discovered wormhole near Saturn to search for a new habitable planet for humanity, forcing him to leave his children behind in a race against time and the vastness of space.
