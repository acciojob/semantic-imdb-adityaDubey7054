Plot Summary

The Matrix tells the story of Thomas Anderson, a computer programmer who leads a double life as the hacker "Neo." He is contacted by Morpheus, a mysterious figure who reveals a startling truth: the world Neo knows is a complex computer simulation called the Matrix, created by intelligent machines to enslave humanity. Neo is believed to be "The One," a prophesied hero destined to liberate mankind.
