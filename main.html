"Game of Thrones" is a sprawling epic centered on the struggle for control of the Iron Throne in the fictional continent of Westeros. The series features multiple plotlines involving various noble families vying for power, interwoven with themes of honor, justice, revenge, and familial bonds. Characters navigate a world of political intrigue, betrayal, and war, facing moral dilemmas and making difficult choices in their quest for power.

Main Actors of Pulp Fiction (1994)

Quentin Tarantino's masterpiece featured a stellar ensemble cast that revitalized several careers. The main players included:

  • Samuel L. Jackson as Jules Winnfield
  • Uma Thurman as Mia Wallace
  • Bruce Willis as Butch Coolidge
